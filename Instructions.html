Instructions

The instructions to this game are fairly simple. You will be given a random secret word
that you must guess letter by letter. You guess the first letter, if correct then
it places the letter in the correct places of the word, but if wrong, it adds a part
to a image of a stickman hanging. Your goal is to ensure the image is not fully built
because that would mean that the man died and you lost. So are you ready to save the man?
